LatestSoccerNews
http://sal006.salnetwork.com:83/Gwen/LatestSoccerNews/CSoccerNews.xml
Provides a recordset of up to date soccer articles from around the world. Contains a headline and URL to the full story. UPDATED: A new method has been added to this service - GetLatestSoccerNewsXML - this returns the soccer news that is returned from the GetLatestSoccerNews method but rather than returning a ByRef Recordset, the data is returned as URLEncoded XML as a string.
