Get Extended Quote
http://www.xignite.com/xquotes.asmx?WSDL
Returns an extended 20 minutes delayed quote for a US Domestic   equity. This includes information such as 52 Week High, 52 Week Low,   P/E Ratio, Market Capitalization as well as the most recent news   headlines. If the security is not traded on date, the service returns   data for the closest trade date.
