IndonesianEconomicIndicator
http://www.indobiz.com/IndoEconoCator.asmx?WSDL
Indonesia economic indicator web service is web service that provides   economic indicators that collected through multiple online resources.   Indicators include Jakarta Stock Exchange index,  Rupiah foreign   exchange rates, bank interest rates, and consumer price index. Data   are available in the form off current and historical data.
