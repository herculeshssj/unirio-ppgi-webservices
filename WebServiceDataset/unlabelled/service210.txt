FastWeather
http://ws2.serviceobjects.net/fw/FastWeather.asmx?WSDL
DOTS FastWeather is a hosted, programmable XML Web Service that   allows you to integrate weather information, forecasts and other   meteorlogic intelligence into your applications, business processes,   and Web sites.  DOTS FastWeather drives return visits to generate   additional page views .    FastWeather will determine the visitor's weather by IP address, zip   code or city/state combination.
