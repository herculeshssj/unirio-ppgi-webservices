WSMaze
http://www.culand.net/WebServices/bin/WSMaze_Server.dll/wsdl/IWSMazeServer
This Web service serves a small multi-player game. It represents a   small virtual maze that users can visit onto meet other visitors of   the maze and compete with them !    The demo client (Windows only) is implemented as an ActiveX control   displaying the scene for the player (using DirectX). Each player can   move into the maze seeking a white post. The maze can accept 5   concurrent players that compete together to earn points.
