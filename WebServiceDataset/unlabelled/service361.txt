XMethods Filesystem
http://www.xmethods.net/sd/2001/XMethodsFilesystemService.wsdl
Virtual Filesystem service with 1MB quota. Allows a client to save, read, and delete string-based files from a &quot;virtual filesystem&quot; hosted on the XMethods server, using a soap interface. For more information, please see the service home page above.
