great circle distance
http://samples.bowstreet.com/bowstreet5/webengine/xmethods/gcd/Action!getWSDL
This service provides two ways to determine the great circle distance   between 2 points of (latitude, longitude). The first operation takes   4 doubles and returns a double. The second operation takes a complex   type and returns a complex type. Latitude values range from -90 to   90, Longitude from -180 to 180.
