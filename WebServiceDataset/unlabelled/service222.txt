The XMLPoll Web Service allows developers to create, use and manage   polls that will be stored on our site and always accessible using   methods and properties provided by it. Once you have created your   own account using our web pages at   http://hosting.msugs.ch/xmlpollweb/default.aspx or calling the   CreateAccount() method provided by the Web Service you will be ready   to use all the common and advanced functionalities typical of a   service useful to manage a poll. You will be able to create a new   poll with a single or multiple answer selection, suspend, archive   and see report on your own polls. Moreover, the Web Service will   provide really useful functionalities such as random poll   visualization.
