Expression Evaluator
http://geb.kattare.com:10130/axis/services/EvaluationService?wsdl
The Expression Evaluator service uses the Java Expression Parser   engine (http://jep.sourceforge.net/) to evaluate complex arithmetic   and algebraic expressions.  Evaluated formulae can include   variables, constants, and functions.      An example formula might be:  2x + 3y^2 - ln(z), x=-8, y=-2, z=20 (result: -6.99573227355399)
