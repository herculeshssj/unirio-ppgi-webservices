Slashdot News Feed
http://webservices.isitedesign.com/ws/slashdotnews.cfc?wsdl
Slashdot provides their newsfeed in XML format for the world to use.    However, you are not allowed to access that XML document more than   once every 30 minutes.  This webservice keeps a 30 minute rolling   cache of the latest headlines from Slashdot, allowing the user to   access the headlines as often as they wish.
