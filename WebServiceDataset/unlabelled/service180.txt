DomainSpy
http://ws2.serviceobjects.net/ds/domainspy.asmx?WSDL
Introducing DOTS DomainSpy, an XML-based domain database that allows   you to identify domain registrants by region or registrant name.     DOTS DomainSpy is a catalog of over 7 million domain registrants in   the US. DOTS DomainSpy provides you with powerful demographics such   as: registrant name, address, city, state, zip code, phone number,   fax numbers, web site, email and much more!
