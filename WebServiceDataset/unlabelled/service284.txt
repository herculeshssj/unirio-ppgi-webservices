Number To Words
http://www2.tankebolaget.com:8080/scripts/NumToWords.dll/wsdl/INumToWords
Converts a number to words, for instance 1001 -&gt; one thousand and one  There are two versions, one English and one Swedish  - The English version accepts a number and returns the number in   words  - The Swedish version takes an additional boolean parameter which,   when set to true, strips spaces from the resulting sentence
