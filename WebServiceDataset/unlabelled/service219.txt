FastQuote
http://ws2.serviceobjects.net/sq/FastQuote.asmx?WSDL
FastQuote is a hosted, programmable XML Web Service that allows you   to integrate stock price, stock volume, and other financial   intelligence into your applications, business processes, and Web   sites.     DOTS FastQuote XML-centric content delivery system allows you to   leverage all of the features and functionality of a hosted solution,   while satisfying your need for a customizable investor releations   Web site.
