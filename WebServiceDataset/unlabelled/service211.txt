Muse.Net Client Service
http://clientservice.muse.net/ClientService.asmx?WSDL
Muse.Net is an XML Web service for personal digital music   management.  Access, control and playback digital media from any   Muse.Net enabled Internet PC or device.     Developers are invited to write Muse.Net Clients (Muses) by   programming against the Web Service directly.  Additionally, Muse.Net   is offering SDKs and sample applications in various languages under   the LGPL to make developing against the Muse.Net Client Web Service   even easier.  For more information, please see http://muse.net/dev   and http://muse.net/faq
