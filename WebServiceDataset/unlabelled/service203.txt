Asian earthquakes
http://webservices.tei.or.th/getQuakeData.cfc?wsdl
Service provides earthquake data within area bounded by -15.33, 59.7   and 49.5,165.3 (DD, decimal degrees). Data is supplied as either XML   or WDDX packets. Required input arguements are &quot;weeks&quot; for how many   weeks worth of quake data in the past you want returned and   WDDXorXMLFlavor for which type of data package you want returned (XML   or WDDX). Returned data packets contain: quake date, quake location,   latitude (DD), longitude (DD), quake origin time, quake depth,   magnitude, magnitude unit, distance (km from Bangkok, Thailand).
