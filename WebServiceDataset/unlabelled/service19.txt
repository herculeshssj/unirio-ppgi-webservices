Get NPV Adjusted for Life Expectancy
http://www.xignite.com/xretirement.asmx?WSDL
Get the Net Present Value of a set of cash flows adjusted for the   life expectancy of an individual.     Three operations are provided to support fixed or variable cash   flows and to allow variable cash flows to be submitted as a comma-  delimitted string or as an array of values.
