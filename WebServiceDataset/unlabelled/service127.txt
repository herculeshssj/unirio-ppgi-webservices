iBeat4D
http://194.98.194.111/4dwsdl
This service return the iBeat of a given hour. If no hour is passed   as parameter, the hour of the SOAP server will be returned.  iBeat is the universal time created by Swatch to have a same time   reference over the world.  For more information about Beat, take a look at the official web   Swatch site: http://www.swatch.com/fs_index.php?  haupt=specials&amp;unter=synchro
