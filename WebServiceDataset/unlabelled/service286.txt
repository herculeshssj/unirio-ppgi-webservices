Monthly Car Payment
http://demo.eltegra.com/webservices/wsdl?app=CarPayment
This EXADEL Web Services implementation can calculate a monthly   car payment. In this simple case, the principal amount of   the loan, the length of the loan's term (number of months), and the   interest rate are required fields.    Please check our Web Services Client Tool at:   http://webservices.eltegra.com
