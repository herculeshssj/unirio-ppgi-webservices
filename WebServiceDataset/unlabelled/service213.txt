Shipping Comparison
http://ws2.serviceobjects.net/pc/packcost.asmx?WSDL
ServiceObjects' DOTS for Shipping Comparison is a hosted,   programmable XML Web Service that allows you to easily compare rates   and services among multiple carriers, including UPS, DHL, FedEx   Express, FedEx Ground, Airborne Express and the US Postal Service.     Features:  International and Domestic pricing for DHL, FedEx, FedEx Ground, UPS,   and USPS.  Compatible with DOTS for Package Tracking.  Dimensional weight enabled.  Fast multi-threaded performance.  DOTS for Shipping allows you to integrate precise and detailed   shipping information - all in one easy-to-access, hosted XML service.
