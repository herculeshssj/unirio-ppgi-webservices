GeoPinpoint
http://ws2.serviceobjects.net/gpp/GeoPinPoint.asmx?WSDL
As soon as someone hits your site, GeoPinpoint will determine the   visitor's geographic location, and makes that information available   throughout the session.    How It Works: The GeoPinpoint detects the IP address of the user,   and   translates that to geographic area. Accuracy of 95% at city level.
