EqImage
http://eu.webmatrixhosting.net/otourdes/EqImageSharp/EqImage.asmx?WSDL
Comparison of two pictures (any format) given by their url.  It returns a double:  - 0 means that pictures are equals;  - a value near 0 means that pictures are closer;  - -1 means that there is an error (image not loaded).
