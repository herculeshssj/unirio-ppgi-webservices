Email Validation
http://ws2.serviceobjects.net/ev/EmailValidate.asmx?WSDL
Validating an email address is surprisingly complex. You need to   write a lot of tricky networking code if you want to check the   syntax, domain and the SMTP server.  Introducing DOTS for Email   Validation, a powerful XML-based email validation solution that   allows your company to instantly verify email address.     You are assured that only legitimate email addresses are saved and   stored in your CRM or other tracking system. This increases the   quality and reduces time and cost for follow-up emailing.    DOTS? for Email Validation identifies free email accounts. Under   certain circumstances, web masters may choose to handle free email   accounts differently. For instance, free email accounts are more   commonly associated with credit card fraud.
