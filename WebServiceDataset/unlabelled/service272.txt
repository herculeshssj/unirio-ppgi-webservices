Web Service Advertising Ring
http://www.soapengine.com/lucin/salcentral/cadvert.asmx?wsdl
Individuals and companies can purchase advertising space within the   Advertising Ring using this web service. The service also allows   advertisers/administrators to view the status of existing campaigns   along with their individual response rates, start date    Example of how to use:    1. Use the following GetUserID method to get yourself a unique   identity number and password.    http://www.soapengine.com/lucin/salcentral/cadvert.asmx?op=GetUserID    2. Get all adverts that appear on the left of the screen using the   GetLeftAdverts method.    http://localhost/lucin/salcentral/cadvert.asmx?op=GetLeftAdvert
