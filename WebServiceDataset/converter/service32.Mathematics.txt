Weight
http://sal006.salnetwork.com:83/a1vbcode/Weight/CWeight.xml
Converts weights from unit of measurement to another. Units for conversion are: Grams, Kilograms, Ounces or Pounds. Methods are available to convert from any one of these units of measures to any of the other units of measure, for example OuncesToGrams, PoundsToKilograms etc.
