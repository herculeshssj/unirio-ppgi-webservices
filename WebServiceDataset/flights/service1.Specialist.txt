AdvancedTravelService
http://www.iternum.com/developer/webservices/advancedtravel/wsdl/advancedtravel.wsdl
This is a demonstration of aggregating several public webservices to provide a new value added service. This returns flight information, weather and stock data for a certain flight request. So you are informed of the current weather and have an idea whether the airline you are going to travel on will still be in business at the time of travel.
